DPDP Act 2023
✅

User Consent

Organizations must obtain clear and explicit user consent for data processing.

👁️

Transparency

Users must be fully informed about how their data is collected and utilized.

🎯

Purpose Limitation

Data can strictly only be used for the specific purpose stated at collection.

🛠️

User Rights

Empowering individuals to access, modify, or withdraw their consent at any time.

🏢

Accountability

Organizations are legally responsible for the security and protection of personal data.

"DPDP Act 2023 empowers individuals with control over their personal data while ensuring organizational accountability."
