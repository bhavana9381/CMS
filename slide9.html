Consent Lifecycle

End-to-End Journey of User Consent in the CMS Platform

Step 01
📋
Consent Collection
User provides explicit and granular consent for specific purposes.
Step 02
🛡️
Consent Validation
System verifies authenticity and legal validity of the request.
Step 03
🗄️
Consent Storage
Encrypted record is stored in the centralized immutable store.
Step 04
⚙️
Consent Usage
Data processed strictly within the boundaries of granted consent.
Step 05
🔄
Update / Renewal
Lifecycle maintenance for preference updates or periodic renewals.
Step 06
↩️
Consent Withdrawal
Seamless mechanism for users to revoke consent as per DPDP Act.
Step 07
📊
Audit Logging
Comprehensive logging of all actions for regulatory evidence.
